FFP Academy

FFP Academy is a 12 week coding boot camp that focuses on mobile app development and website development. We focus on creating an immersive coding boot camp experience for individuals that are looking to get their foot in the door within the technology space. Benefit from a wide range of career services to position you for success through graduation and beyond. With us, you can build a portfolio of web applications to showcase your knowledge.

This program is for developers of all experience levels to enhance their development and behavioral skills. Over this time period, our team will teach you all the necessary tools needed in order to get hired by top firms in the country. During your time here, you will be exposed to real-world application building and training with industry professionals that are familiar with the startup space and culture. We don't just focus on the development aspect but also focus on the interpersonal and behavioral aspect of the hiring process - starting from applications to personal and technical interviews.

Upon graduation - you'll gain ongoing routine check-ins from our teams to help smoothen the job hunting process. You'll also have access to our curated network of employers looking to hire Coding Boot Camp graduates.
